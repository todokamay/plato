## Threshold Alignment

The bitrate thresholds are now centralized in `config.THRESHOLDS`:

- `very_low_video_bitrate_kbps = 1500`
- `min_video_bitrate_kbps = 2500`
- `preferred_video_bitrate_kbps = 3500`

These are reused by:

- raw technical scoring: `modules/scoring.py` lines 74-103
- verdict caps: `modules/scoring.py` lines 268-306
- adjusted-score consistency penalty banding: `modules/score_consistency.py` line 120
- report threshold display: `modules/report_generator.py` lines 68-70

No mismatch was found between the cap threshold and the edit-point threshold for low bitrate. The edit point receives the already-classified scoring issue rather than reading another hardcoded bitrate value.

## Intentional Differences

The bitrate ladder intentionally has three severities:

- Below 1500 kbps: very low bitrate, stronger score penalty, cap to REWORK.
- 1500-2500 kbps: low bitrate, score penalty, cap to SAFE TO TEST.
- 2500-3500 kbps: acceptable but below preferred, smaller score penalty, cap only prevents STRONG PUBLISH by capping to PUBLISH.

This difference is a severity ladder, not a threshold mismatch.
